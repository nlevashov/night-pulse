# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Night Pulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Health Data
Night Pulse accesses the following data from Apple HealthKit:
- Sleep analysis data (sleep stages: Core, Deep, REM)
- Heart rate measurements during sleep periods

This data is used solely to generate sleep reports and is processed entirely on your device.

### Data You Provide
- Email addresses of report recipients (for Gmail delivery)
- Telegram bot tokens and chat IDs (for Telegram delivery)

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not store your health data on external servers

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:
1. Generate daily sleep heart rate reports
2. Deliver reports to recipients you specify via Gmail or Telegram
3. Display your sleep statistics within the app

## Data Storage

- **Health data** is stored locally on your device using secure storage
- **Authentication tokens** (Gmail, Telegram) are stored in the device's secure keychain
- **No data is transmitted to our servers** â€” we do not operate any backend servers

## Third-Party Services

Night Pulse integrates with the following third-party services at your discretion:

### Google Gmail API
- Used only when you enable Gmail delivery
- Sends emails from your own Gmail account
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Telegram Bot API
- Used only when you enable Telegram delivery
- Sends messages through your own Telegram bot
- Subject to [Telegram's Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Health data is only shared with recipients you explicitly configure (via email or Telegram).

## Data Retention

- Health data is retained on your device until you delete the app
- You can clear all stored data at any time through the app settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data stored within the app
- Delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
- Revoke HealthKit permissions through iOS Settings
- Disconnect Gmail or Telegram integrations at any time

## Children's Privacy

Night Pulse is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Secure storage for sensitive credentials
- No transmission of health data to external servers
- Local-only data processing
